---

## A Disclaimer Before Anything Else

I am allergic to alcohol. Completely. So if you have ever met me and thought "this person's humor is a little unhinged" — it was not because I was drunk. I have never been drunk. I am physically incapable of it.

What I am capable of is a sugar rush.

Give me a boba, an energy drink, a second dessert, or anything with more than 40 grams of sugar and I will start making jokes that I think are hilarious and you will not know what to do with. I will send voice notes that go in four different directions. I will come up with a bit that only makes sense if you were there. I will photograph myself in a lift and send it to a group chat with no context.

![This is one of those photos. I sent this to someone. I don't remember why.](images/selfie-lift.jpg)

That is me. That is the full picture.

## What This Says About Me Professionally

Probably that I am a normal person who does serious work and also has a chaotic side that only activates under specific sugary conditions.

I think that is fine. Maybe even good. The ability to switch between drafting a policy brief for parliament and sending a completely unhinged voice note to a friend at midnight is, in my opinion, a sign of a balanced life.

I work in government relations. I attend formal meetings. I write documents that go to ministries. And sometimes, after a good dessert, I become a slightly different person for about forty-five minutes.

---

## Why This Article Exists

Because a portfolio that is only serious is a portfolio that is only half-honest.

The research is real. The experience is real. The work I put into everything else on this site is real. But so is this. The person who did all of that also sends random selfies from lifts and makes jokes that require too much explanation.

If that combination interests you as a colleague or collaborator — genuinely, let's talk.

If not, I respect that completely, and I hope the rest of the portfolio was useful.

Either way: do not give me an energy drink at a work event. You have been warned.
